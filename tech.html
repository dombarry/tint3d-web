Our Technology

At Tint3d, we pride ourselves on the innovative deployments of the technologies we employ. Using dye to alter objects is something humans have done for centuries, but this is something brand new.

Heat and Dye Process

Our unique process starts with a heated dye bath. The combination of heat and pressure ensures an even coating. Moreover, our state-of-the-art jet system eliminates the risk of scratching, providing pristine parts every time.

Drying Process

After dyeing, the solution is automatically drained, and each part undergoes a thorough rinse to remove excess dye. We finalize the process with a dry heating step, ensuring the dye sets perfectly.

Innovative Material

Our proprietary material guarantees not only fantastic color results but also ensures the parts have remarkable mechanical strength and longevity.
