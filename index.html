Raw SLA Parts

Raw SLA parts have great mechanical properties, but they are held back by their lack of color.

Heat and Dye Process

Parts are submerged in heated dye. They are moved around in this solution to ensure even dye.

Drying Process

Finally, the dye is automatially drained out, and the part is dried and sprayed down to remove excess dye.
