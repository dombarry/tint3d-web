Raw SLA Parts

Raw SLA parts have great mechanical properties, but they are held back by their lack of color. For companies that are looking to use their parts for focus groups, real world testing or as end use products it is important to have lifelike parts.

Heat and Dye Process

Your parts begin their process in our machines in a warmed and preassure treated dye bath. The heat and pressure helps insure even coating, and our jet system moves parts around without the fear of scratching them.

Drying Process

Finally, the dye is automatially drained out, and the part is dried and sprayed down to remove excess dye. A dry heating process is quickly done to set the dye in the part. All of these steps are acheived in one machine to further simplify your workflow.
